PROMO SCAM

Difficulty to Spot: Easy

+ SHOW MORE

The linker wants you to email them, or go to their cam page, or go to their personal web site. The accounts are almost always displaying female photos. These type of bots generally target men, who are more likely to swipe right to a suggestive account than women are to male accounts. It doesn't matter what they say; they just want you to click on their virus-laden malware link. Obviously, don't do that. Beware of any link a stranger sends to you on a dating platform.

Commentary

In 2014, many users complained about being matched with fake profiles of women which were actually automated bots promoting a mobile game called “Castle Clash.”
