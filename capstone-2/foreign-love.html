FOREIGN LOVE

Difficulty to Spot: Medium

+ SHOW MORE

You may come across a match who claims to be from a different country. Watch out. The scammers almost always claim that their aim is to find love and to build a connection, sometimes telling their victims that finding one another was "destiny."

The truth, of course, could not be farther from this. They cast nets far and wide to find people who are most vulnerable and willing to believe their story. Often times, these scammers attempt to find women who are middle-aged or older, single or widowed and potentially in a vulnerable state. They then prey on the person's insecurities and hope to find a genuine connection.

Commentary

Right off the bat, a scammer will shower you with complements. A lot of complements.
