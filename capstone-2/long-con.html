THE LONG CON

Difficulty to Spot: Hard

+ SHOW MORE

Possibly the most clever and paranoia-inducing of all romance scammers. The 'long con' is a term used by hustlers and con artists to describe a long-term operation against a particular person. The scammer is not trying to get anything from the victim right away, or even in the near future. Instead, they are collecting information and laying groundwork for a much larger scheme. They may go out on dates with you. They may actually have a relationship with you. All of this is part of a larger con aimed at getting access to your identity or your assets, especially if you are a wealthy person.

Commentary

Ethan, a 29 year-old, met Cris on Tinder around four months ago. Cris's profile showed he was a jet setting, successful businessman with a charming personality, so they started chatting casually before moving to Whatsapp and having more deep and meaningful conversations. Over time, a relationship started to form.
